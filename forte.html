Forte

Web Developer


Forte is a Berkeley nonprofit that strives to make music lessons more affordable for children from underserved communities. They match K-12 students with college-age teachers, and offer over 12 instruments.

The Problem

As Forte scales their operations to cater to more students and teachers, they can no longer manage to do everything manually, including accepting applications, matching up suitable teachers with students, and scheduling individual music lessons.

The Solution

We built a web application to help automate a large part of this process. This web application will include an application system for interested students and teachers, a scheduling dashboard for students and teachers to see upcoming and past lessons, and profiles for individual students and teachers. Through this system, Forte will be able to handle more demand, ultimately fostering lifelong passions for music.
