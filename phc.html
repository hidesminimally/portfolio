Project Homeless Connect

Mobile Developer


The Project Homeless Connect (PHC) Mobile Application is currently in the app store and is being used at PHC's five service events every year by about a hundred volunteers per event. During the spring semester of 2015, I worked alongside Blueprint developers as an android developer to ship this application. This application saves at least 200 volunteer hours per event, significantly improves the consistency and hygiene of the information in their database by removing duplicate data sets and tracks about half a dozen new data points per client that can be used to help improve and target offered services
