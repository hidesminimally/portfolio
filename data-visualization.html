Data Visualization Framework on VR systems

Mobile Application


At the AT&T VR Mobile App Hackathon during the summer of 2015, I designed an immersive virtual reality framework for real-time data visualization on Gear VR with Unity Game Engine with a team of two other UC Berkeley engineers. The framework enables users to literally walk into big data represented by dots and curves, zoom, pinch and travel in 4 dimensions
